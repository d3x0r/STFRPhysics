Physics

RETARDED Σ: The displacement field Σ(x,t) satisfies a wave equation at speed c. Each body's contribution is evaluated at the retarded position — where light-cone intersection places it. The field carries memory: the gradient at any point reflects where sources were, not where they are.

The retarded position is found by iterative fixed-point solve: find t_ret such that |x_source(t_ret) − x_field| = c·(t − t_ret).
